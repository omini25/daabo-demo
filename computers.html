Computer Protection

Get protection for Desktop computers.

What We Offer?

The full protection we are offering is specifically designed to cover your desktop computer consiting of the monitor and pc.

Broken or Cracked Monitor Screen

We cover your monitor if your screen gets broken or cracked.

Liquid Damages

We cover your pc against damage from accidentally spilling liquid on it.

Accidental Damages Repair

We cover your pc against accidental damages.

International Cover

We know that accidents can occur anywhere. Our protection cover extends beyound your home country.

Theft And Accidental Loss

We cover your computer if someone pinches it from you or if it is stolen from a locked house or car. And also if it is accidentally lost.

Power surges due to lightning

We provide cover agaist damage caused by power surges due to lightning.

What We Do Not Cover?

Computer protection doesn't cover everything. Some exclusions are:

Computer viruses

Wear and tear or corrosion

Software issues

Blatant neglect

Signup

Benefits/offerings

Daabo will pay up to 35% of the cost of the device in the event that the screen of your device is broken.
Daabo will pay up to 25% of the cost of the device in the event that the device is damaged.
Daabo will pay up to 65% of the cost of the device in the event of total loss of your device which may be as a result of theft or accidental loss.

  • E.g. Cost of device = N30,000.00 Daabo will pay = N10,500.00 to repair your damaged screen
  • E.g. Cost of phone = N200,000.00 Daabo will pay = N130,000.00 to replace your lost device
